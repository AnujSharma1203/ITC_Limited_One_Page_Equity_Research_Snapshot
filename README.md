# 📊 ITC Limited | One-Page Equity Research Snapshot

> Turning complex financial data into clear, analyst-ready insights.

---

## 🔍 About the Project
This repository showcases a **one-page equity research snapshot of ITC Limited**, created as part of structured learning with **The Valuation School**.

The objective was to **analyze financial performance, valuation metrics, ownership structure, and market trends**, and present them in a **concise, professional one-page format** suitable for quick screening and informed equity analysis.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
✔ Convert large financial datasets into structured insights  
✔ Apply **fundamental equity research and valuation techniques**  
✔ Build an **analyst-style one-page company profile**

## 📈 What’s Covered
- Company overview & business segments  
- Multi-year financial performance (Revenue, Margins, EPS, Dividends)  
- Valuation analysis using **P/E, EV/EBITDA, P/B, EV/Sales**  
- 5-year share price & volume trend analysis  
- Profitability and efficiency ratios (**ROE, ROCE**)  
- Shareholding pattern & institutional ownership  
- Managerial remuneration overview  
- Capital structure & enterprise value  
- Recent corporate updates  
- Clearly cited data sources
